What C-3BO is capable of

Capabilities
-> Visually detecting peripheral blood smear leukemia
-> Differentiating acute myeloid leukemia, acute lymphoblastic leukemia, and healthy patients
-> Gene sequencing combination of BCR-ABL1 features
-> Accurately stating the clinical classification of the Philadelphia chromosome
Technologies Involved & Compatibility
-> Jupyter Notebook for original network training
-> Tensorflow, Keras, and Scikit Learn
-> Matplotlib and Seaborn for visualization results
-> V.1.1 Tkinter for binary classification of tumors
-> Beyond V.1.1 Python's Kivy framework
